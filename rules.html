NBA Playoffs Fantasy — Rules

← Scoreboard

Objective

Draft eight NBA players — one for each playoff seed (1–8) — and accumulate the most points across the full postseason. Most total points at the end of the NBA Finals wins.

Roster Selection

  • Each entrant picks 8 players, one per seed 1–8. A seed-slot pick may come from either the Eastern or Western Conference playoff team at that seed.
  • Different entrants may pick the same player.
  • Only players on the 16 teams that qualify for the playoffs post-play-in are eligible. Play-in rounds don't count.
  • Submit a roster via the "Enter Picks" button on the scoreboard.

Scoring

Per round

A player's score in a round is the average of their top-4 games that round (top-4 PPG). Four rounds total: Round 1, Conference Semifinals, Conference Finals, NBA Finals.

round_score = average(top-4 games by points) × seed_multiplier

If a player plays fewer than 4 games in a round, the average is over however many they played.

Seed multiplier

Each seed-slot has a multiplier equal to 1 + seed/10. Higher seeds score bigger.

seed multiplier: 1-seed = 1.1×, 2-seed = 1.2×, … 8-seed = 1.8×

Historical note: the seed multiplier only applies from 2025 onward. 2022–2024 scoring used raw round PPGs (no multiplier).

Example: Andrew Nembhard is a 4-seed. He scores 10 points in a game → that game counts as 10 × 1.4 = 14 toward his round average.
Worked example: Shai Gilgeous-Alexander (1-seed) goes for 33, 34, 18, 25, 31, 32, 35 in a 7-game series. Top-4 average = (33 + 34 + 32 + 35) / 4 = 33.5 PPG. That round's contribution: 33.5 × 1.1 = 36.85 points.

Entrant total

entrant_total = Σ round_score across all 4 rounds, summed over all 8 picks

Scoreboard Columns

  • Total: your cumulative fantasy points.
  • +/-: how much each pick is out-performing its cost. Per pick, (actual average PPG) − cost; summed across your 8 picks. Green = exceeding cost, red = underperforming.
  • Alive: picks still in the playoffs, out of 8.
  • PPG Rem: expected remaining upside. Σ cost × multiplier across alive picks — roughly how many points you'd earn next round if everyone ran at their season PPG.
  • Seed cells: each cell shows the seed · multiplier, player headshot, name, and current fantasy total. Hover for per-game stats.
